Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day to maintain proper hydration and support bodily functions.

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night to support physical and mental health.

Nutritional Guidelines

Daily Recommended Intake

  • Calories: 2000-2500 (varies by individual)
  • Protein: 50-60 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 225-325 grams
  • Fiber: 25-30 grams
  • Fat: 44-78 grams

Key Vitamins and Minerals

  • Vitamin C: 65-90 mg
  • Vitamin D: 600-800 IU
  • Calcium: 1000-1200 mg
  • Iron: 8-18 mg
  • Potassium: 3500-4700 mg

Common Health Conditions

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Maintain a healthy diet low in sodium, exercise regularly, and manage stress to help control blood pressure.

Type 2 Diabetes

Monitor blood sugar levels, maintain a balanced diet, and stay physically active to manage diabetes effectively.

Heart Disease

Adopt a heart-healthy lifestyle with a balanced diet, regular exercise, and stress management techniques.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Control blood pressure, manage diabetes if present, and follow a kidney-friendly diet as recommended by your doctor.

Understanding Your Health Metrics

Blood Pressure

Normal: Below 120/80 mmHg
Elevated: 120-129/<80 mmHg
High: 130/80 mmHg or higher

Body Mass Index (BMI)

Underweight: Below 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5-24.9
Overweight: 25-29.9
Obese: 30 or higher

Healthy Lifestyle Tips

Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week.

Balanced Diet

Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in your daily meals.

Stress Management

Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga to reduce stress and improve overall well-being.
